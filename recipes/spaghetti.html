Spaghetti

Home

Description

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups of Water
  • 1/3 pack of 200mg Spaghetti
  • Salt and additional spices
  • Table spoon of oil

Steps:

  1. Break and fit your spaghetti into a pot.
  2. Pour the three cups of water to your rice.
  3. Pour the table spoon of fish oil.
  4. Add a spoon of salt and additional spices.
  5. Leave to cook for 20 minutes.
  6. Your spaghetti is complete, make sure to drain any remaining water.
