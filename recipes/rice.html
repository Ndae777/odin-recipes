Rice

Home

Description

Ingredients:

  • Three cups of Water
  • A half cup of rice
  • Salt and additional spices

Steps:

  1. You will rinse your rice carefully until starch is less visible.
  2. Pour the three cups of water to your rice.
  3. Add a spoon of salt and additional spices.
  4. Leave to cook for 20 minutes.
  5. Your rice is complete, make sure to drain any remaining water.
